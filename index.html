Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

Overview

BlinkSubs is a subscription tracking app that works entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Data Storage

All subscription data you enter is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export a backup file.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

— No analytics or tracking
— No advertising identifiers
— No personal information
— No usage statistics
— No server-side storage

Zero data collected. Zero data shared.

Notifications

BlinkSubs may send local notifications to remind you about upcoming payments. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and do not involve any external service.

Backup & Export

You may export your subscription data as a JSON file. This file is created and saved locally on your device. We have no access to exported files.

Third-Party Services

BlinkSubs does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising frameworks.

Changes

If we update this policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated date.
